BREAST CANCER-FIGHTING FOODS & diet chart

Healthy foods boost the immune system and help maintain a healthy weight, lowering the risk of cancer. The following are 10 foods that can help fight and prevent breast cancer:
1.Almonds – They are a great source of monounsaturated fats that help prevent breast cancer as well as other typesof cancer.
2.Beans – Eating legumes twice a week can lower the rate of developing breast cancer.
3.Blueberries – They are a great source of antioxidants, including cancer-fighting chlorogenic acid.
4.Broccoli – They are rich in sulforaphane, a plant chemical that interferes with the growth of breast cancer cells. 5.Flaxseed – Eating two tablespoons of ground flaxseed a day can slow the rate of cancer-sell growth.
6.Garlic – Allyl sulfur and other substances in garlic slow the growth of tumor cells.
7.Green tea – It is an excellent source of catechins, which are potent antioxidants. If green tea is sipped daily, the risk of developing breast cancer decreases.
8.Mushrooms – Portobello, cremini, button, and shiitake mushrooms contain ergothioneine and selenium, which are immune-boosting antioxidants that can decrease the riskof cancer.
9.Spinach – It contains the powerful antioxidants betacarotene and lutein. If eaten at least twice a week, the risk of breast cancer decreases.
10.Salmon and Seafood - If 1.5 to 3 ounces of fish or shellfish are consumed daily, the risk of developing breast cancer decreases by 26 percent.








Fight Cervical Cancer With The Right Diet.

A nutritious diet and adequate water input are the best ways to start. At least some meals exist that work to fight cervical cancer and lessen symptoms due to the antioxidant effects and many nutrients they provide.
1.Sweet Potatoes: Many orange and yellow fruits and vegetables possess beta-carotene, and this antioxidant can stem and treat several types of cancer, including the cervical type. Sweet potatoes are one of the most significant sources of beta-carotene are much more nutritious than regular potatoes It’s essential to consume the outer skin portion as most nutrients are found there.
2.Spinach: Spinach consists of dark green leaves containing folic acid, which helps build new cells. These new cells can support pushing out the impaired, toxic cells rousing cervical cancer and other ailments within the body. Spinach is an excellent source of other vitamins, mainly Vitamin E, which enhances healthy cell function.
3.Carrots: Carrots possess tonnes of nutrients that help in the fight against disorders. The antioxidants found in this vegetable are precious in the fight against cervical cancer because they target the human papillomavirus. A diet rich in cancer-fighting foods is a significant benefit.
4.Apples: Apples furnish a hefty dose of Vitamin C, which serves as an antioxidant that can combat the disorder within the body. Many people increase their vitamin C intake when they have a regular cold, not recognizing that the nutritional value can help with other ailments, including cervical cancer. It’s a true saying that an apple a day keeps the doctor away.
5.Eggs: Protein is significant for healing the body and restoring impaired tissues. Eggs are an ideal source of protein, making them an important food to eat during a fight against cancer of any sort.
6.Pink Grapefruit: The key combination for cervical health here is lycopene, and it belongs to the exact carotenoid family as beta-carotene.
8.Broccoli: Broccoli has vitamin B, well known as folate, which is usually given to women to secure the health of their reproductive system during the childbearing duration. So it is understood that folate could also aid in holding the cervix in shape.
9.Bell peppers: Evidence indicates that bell pepper is rich in phenolic compounds and flavonoids, responsible for lessening cancer episodes. Vitamin C is great at sustaining a healthy immune system preventing HPV infections.
